This is a 3-lingual voabulary trainer.

It is designed as "personal" vocabulary trainer. 

You can add new words to your vocabulary and make vocabulary-tests.
You can search for foreign words and they will be displayed in your motherlanguage and English. 

If your motherlanguage is English, just choose any other language you want to learn in the settings menu.

Currently implemented languages: Arabic, French, German, Italian, Romanian, Russian, Spanish, Turkish.
